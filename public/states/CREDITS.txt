State location-tile photos — provenance & licensing
====================================================

The state-image completeness pass added the 14 files documented below. Source
images were downloaded from Wikimedia Commons, center-cropped, resized to
600x450, stripped of source metadata, and recompressed for small directory tiles.

Twelve additions are Public Domain. Connecticut and Vermont are licensed under
CC BY-SA 4.0; the modified files remain available under that same license. All
14 sources permit commercial reuse under the terms recorded below.
